A kitchen born
from slow living.

Maison Noir started with one conviction — that food and coffee deserve the same level of obsession. We source ingredients from local farms, roast our beans weekly, and bake everything in-house before you arrive each morning.

The Bean

Direct-trade micro-lots from Coorg, Chikmagalur, and Ethiopia. Roasted in-house weekly in small batches.

The Kitchen

Open kitchen, seasonal menu. We change dishes based on what's fresh at the farm — not what's convenient.

The Space

Designed to make time slow down. Natural light, handmade ceramics, and chairs you won't want to leave.
